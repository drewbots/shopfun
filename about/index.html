About Us

We started Surf n Sol in 2009 to share our love of the ocean and a balanced lifestyle on the island of Kauai. Several years later, we expanded our services to the island of Oahu to offer a wider range of fun and affordable options. Our focus is providing a tailored vacation experience to each of our guests, centered around surfing and yoga, as well as the natural beauty offered by our islands. Our expert team is ready to help you plan and enjoy the perfect Hawaii adventure while sharing the spirit of aloha.
