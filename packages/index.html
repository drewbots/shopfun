Packages and Pricing

Please contact us via email if you would like to receive a price quote.

Try to include:

  • Which location? Waikiki or Kauai
  • Travel dates
  • The number of travelers in your party
  • The number of bedrooms required
  • Some information about the activities you’d like to do

If you don’t know all of this information, don’t worry!
Get in touch with us, and we’re happy to help you come up with a plan.
